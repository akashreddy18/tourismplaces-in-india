Varanasi Temple

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. It stands on the western bank of the holy Ganga River and is considered one of the 12 Jyotirlingas. and Varanasi also known as Kashi or Banaras. Varanasi is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world over 3,000 years old and mentioned in ancient scriptures.

NearBy Places to Visit:

  • Sarnath
  • Ramnagar Fort
  • Chunar Fort
